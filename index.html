Protecting Our Seas, Empowering Our Future

Solwara Youth Guardian (SYG INC) is dedicated to marine conservation and youth empowerment in Papua New Guinea. Together, we can create a sustainable future for our oceans and communities.

About Us

Our Story

Solwara Youth Guardian was founded by passionate young leaders who recognized the urgent need to protect our marine ecosystems while empowering the next generation of environmental stewards. Born from a deep love for our oceans and a commitment to positive change, we believe that youth are the key to a sustainable future.

The Problem

Climate change, marine pollution, and a gap in youth involvement threaten our oceans. Rising sea levels, plastic pollution, and declining marine biodiversity are real challenges that affect our communities. We're bridging this gap by engaging young people in conservation efforts and creating awareness about these critical issues.

Our Mission

To protect marine ecosystems through youth-led conservation initiatives, education, and community engagement. We strive to create a movement where every young person feels empowered to take action for our environment.

Our Vision

A future where empowered youth lead the charge in preserving our oceans for generations to come. We envision thriving marine ecosystems, clean beaches, and communities that live in harmony with nature.

Community Impact

Our Beneficiaries

Local communities, youth groups, schools, and volunteer networks across Papua New Guinea. We work with coastal communities, indigenous groups, and young people who are passionate about protecting their environment.

Community Support

Through outreach programs, education initiatives, and collective action, we're building a movement for change. Our programs are designed to empower communities to take ownership of their local environments and create sustainable solutions.

Long-term Goal

Becoming a nationally recognized environmental movement that influences policy and inspires action. We aim to expand our reach across Papua New Guinea and become a leading voice in marine conservation and youth empowerment.
